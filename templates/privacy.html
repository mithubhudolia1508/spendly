Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filtering. We do not use your personal data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

We may use anonymised, aggregated usage data to understand how people use Spendly and to improve the product.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually bound to keep your data confidential.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.
